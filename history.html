The Low Down

Creston, Colorado

Davian, Jared, and Gladys founded Ghost boards out of Creston, Colorado back in 1987, and they have dedicated their life to snowboarding ever since. A passion for the culture and sport keeps them churning out products year after year.

The Ghosty Gnar Promise

Everything we do at Ghost Boards started in the mountains. From getting the most out of every journey to chasing snow around the globe, we've charged ahead to innovate and change the way people enjoy the outdoors since day one.

Mission

Focusing less on excessive promotion and hype, and more on using the highest quality of materials, craftsmanship, and keeping you ready to shred those fresh pow pow days.
